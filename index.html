Welcome to Lazy Control – the future of hands-free computing. Using advanced AI-powered gaze tracking, you can now open apps, scroll pages, and control your laptop with nothing but your eyes and a blink. 🚀 Features

  • 🎯 Gaze-controlled navigation
  • 🔒 Blink to unlock
  • 🎵 Control apps like YouTube, Music, Files etc.

📖 How It Works

Using webcam feed and computer vision, Lazy Control detects your iris position and blink patterns. These actions are mapped to on-screen elements, letting you trigger apps and system actions seamlessly.
